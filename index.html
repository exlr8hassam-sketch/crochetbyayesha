The maker

Hi, I'm Ayesha — I turn yarn into things people fall in love with.

What started as a small hobby is now a little arts & crafts studio spreading love, one stitch at a time. Every bouquet, plushie and keepsake here is handmade to order, which means your piece is made just for you — not pulled off a shelf.

I work in soft, dusty palettes because that's what feels like comfort to me. If you can dream it in yarn, there's a good chance we can crochet it.

How ordering works

🧶
